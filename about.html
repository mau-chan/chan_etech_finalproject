About us

Welcome to Cookies and Charms, a whimsical bakery where delicious treats and charming accessories come together! Step into a world of sweetness and creativity, where every bite is delightful and every bead is a symbol of your unique style.

As you enjoy our delectable treats, immerse yourself in the enchanting world of beaded accessories. We offer an array of handmade jewelry and accessories, crafted with love and attention to detail. Express your individuality and create memories as you browse through our collection of charms, beads, and trinkets. And for those who wish to unleash their creativity, we provide a unique opportunity to design your own beaded accessories with the guidance of our friendly staff. From bracelets to necklaces, let your imagination run wild and create a personalized piece that reflects your style and captures the essence of your charm.

So, come and experience the magic at Cookies and Charms. Treat yourself to scrumptious cakes, indulge in artisanal bread, sip on delightful drinks, and explore the world of beaded accessories. We invite you to embrace the sweetness, charm, and creativity that await you in our cozy bakery.
